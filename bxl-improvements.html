Some quick saving of info I see about improving Brussels. Mostly from a pedestrian, runner, biking, or public transport view.

  • A12 motorway into Brussels to become green boulevard. Project "Parkway 21". Persbericht Brussel. Bruzz artikel. In short, that big street at my far side of Park van Laken, the end of the A12. Is very unfriendly for people. They want to reduce the car lanes and improve pedestrian/biking access and add more green. Would be that area from the big roundabout to where currently the Heysel covid test center is. The road down towards Van Praet would also be improved.

    Not everyone agrees with this, of course. My first question also was "How will they handle the car traffic that currently uses it?". This indeed seems the main complaint, the plan does not take it into account. Smet neemt plans voor groenere A12 onder vuur.

  • Wallonië timmert aan vijf fietssnelwegen naar Brussel. Brussels times article. Five bike highways from Wallonia to Brussels: Tubize to mid Anderlecht, Braine l'Alleud to just past Linkebeek, Waterloo to Frelaan along Ter Kamerenbos, Ottignies via La Hulpe to that road passing Heiligenborre into Watermaal, and Waver along big road past Overijse past Jezus-eik onto Waverse steenweg.
  • Jette krijg half miljoen van Gewest om verder te vergroenen. More green next to the cemetery (tram terminus will be removed). More plants / letting rain into the ground in Odon Warlandlaan and Liebrechtlaan.
  • Gewest lanceert gesprekken voor grondige heraanleg Tervurenlaan. They want to turn the Tervurenlaan into a "city boulevard". Fewer cars, more bike, more pedestrians. Goal for permit: Octobre 2022. Other: Gewest en Etterbeek zoeken compromis over Tervurenlaan, met breder fietspad aan zuidzijde.
  • A tram (tram 10) planned from Rogier to Neder-over-Heembeek. Recent Bruzz article. Google Maps of the planned route.
  • Zes zwarte kruispunten komende weken aangepakt. Construction planned to be done by early 2022. They list the places in the article. Mostly wider sidewalk/bikepath and better lights.
  • Fietsers en wandelaars krijgen doorgang onder drie drukste kanaalpunten. Construction start planned for 2023. Van Praet brug, De Trooz brug, Sainctelettebrug.
  • Heraanleg Marie Jansonplein start volgende week. Sint-Gillis. Construction ends end 2022. Streets around become autoluw. Square becomes a city park with playground, fountainn, green.
  • Herinrichting Prinses Elisabethlaan. Greener, wider sidewalk, add bike path.
  • Bouw fietssnelweg langs E40 start begin 2022. F203 that already exists in Flanders (to centre Sterrebeek) will be continued for another 500m into Evere. Construction start early 2022. In 2023 the F203 will be continued till Leuven via Bertem. Other plans include continuing the Brussels side better too, but no exact plans exist yet.
  • Twee autovrije pleinen op komst in Broustinlaan. On both ends of Broustinlaan, a street near the Basilica.
  • NegativeVastgoedproject aan Tenreuken zal leiden tot kap 311 bomen
  • Negative. Ukkel wil weer meer verkeer in Ter Kamerenbos en schakelt advocaat in. Ukkel being dicks and want Ter Kamerenbos back for cars.
  • NMBS versterkt aanbod rond Brussel. More S-trains: S9 to Landen, S8 to Zottegem. More trains to Amsterdam.
  • Bûûmplanters gaan Van Praet-site vergroenen. Overstroombaar park, mensluwe zone en gemengde wijk: dit is het masterplan Van Praet. New school, theatre, compact housing at Van Praet. Also planning green: trees / swamp.
  • Omgeving metrostation Delacroix wordt groener. More green and better bike crossing at Delacroix and Abbatoir.
  • Fiets- en voetgangersbrug wordt over Brusselse Ring geplaatst: drie weekends hinder. Bike and pedestrian bridge over the BXL Ring. Connects Zaventem and Machelen. Finishes the bike highway Brussel-Leuven. Should be done after the weekend of 13 February 2022.
  • "Tiny forest" planting started at NOH pool. (31 Jan 2022)
  • New circulation plan in Kuregem (Anderlecht side). Aim: fewer cars. One way streets, traffic filters.
  • Middenstand voelt zich miskend over nieuwe Gulden Vlieslaan. Between Louisa and Naamsepoort: fewer car lanes, more green. Stores complain as always.
  • Dit is het plan voor een autoluwe Vijfhoek. New circulation plan for cars in the pentagon. Goal: discourage driving through it to get to the other side (you should go around on the big roads). Concensus seems to be: step in the right direction, not perfect yet. Eventual goal: execute similar plans in all parts of Brussels.
